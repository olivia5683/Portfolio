OUR SERVICES

Before booking any services we offer a complimentary consultation with Raphaela to ensure we fully understand your specific needs, and can come prepared for the service that you expect. This truly is a complimentary service with no strings attached! If you don't see the specific service you need listed below, please inquire about a custom package to meet your needs.

Cleaning
Creating clarity begins with a clean environment! We are well equipped with cleaning tools and can easily accomodate those who wish to only use non-toxic products. Our cleaning packages range from a one-time deep clean of your home to monthly or weekly packages.
Professional Organization
When you have the things you need and the space to store it, but still can't find a place to put it all, that's where our professional organizers come in! Whether it's your kitchen, bedroom, garage, or office, our organizers are able to optimize your belongings and space for flawless function. We can implement storage solutions, help with moving in & out of a home, organize yard & estate sales, and can provide hoarding support. There is no space we can't tackle!
Personal Chef & Meal Plans
Food is more than fuel: it's comforting, vital to survive, provides community, and it's delicious. Our chef services can help you wherever you're at. We can help you do it all from planning the meals, shopping & cooking, just planning your week or month and you take over the rest, or if you know what you like but want to learn the basics of cooking- our personal chefs have seen and done it all. We love what food has to offer, and we want to make your meal times as stress-free and as enjoyable as possible.
Health & Life Coaching
Your health can dramatically improve with the right information and support. We can help you identify the areas in your life that need change and provide the tools and support you need to get there. We want to see you express your authentic gifts in your life and relationships.
Personal Assistance
Whether it's booking travel plans, managing your work or personal calendar, shopping, or hiring a house sitter, we can all use a little less on our plate. We are here to help you stay organized and on track!
Custom Packages
Don't quite see what you need? That's okay! We are here to listen and to create an individualized plan of action to meet your specific needs. Maybe you have your meals down regularly, but there's family in town and you'd rather spend quality time with them rather than plan activities and meals for 10- we can do that! Maybe you've got a big deadline coming up and it would be helpful to have a couple things from each category delegated to a third party- we've got you there, too. Our mission is to help you have a healthier life at home, whatever that means to you.
